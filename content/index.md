# 0xskeksis

This website is a place where I publish my notes, articles, and technical write-ups.

I use it to document what I learn, keep track of ideas, and share material about programming, computer science, systems, security, and related topics.

The content here is not meant to be perfectly polished. Some pages are structured articles, while others are personal notes written while learning, experimenting, or building projects.

## What you can find here

- Technical notes
- Articles and write-ups
- Project documentation
- Research logs
- Learning notes

## Topics

Most of the content will focus on low-level programming, systems, security, networking, graphics, and software engineering.

## About

I am interested in understanding how things work under the hood, from operating systems and compilers to protocols, rendering pipelines, and security mechanisms.
